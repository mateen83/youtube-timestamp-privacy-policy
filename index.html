Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the YouTube Timestamp Bookmarker extension handles information. We wrote this summary in plain language so it is easy to read. YouTube Timestamp Bookmarker lets you capture, organize, and revisit key moments from any YouTube video with ease. Bookmark timestamps, add notes, categorize them, and enjoy a comfortable dark mode experience.

Product: YouTube Timestamp Bookmarker Version: 1.0 Effective date: 2025-08-03

Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal data. The extension works entirely in your browser and does not transmit any data externally.

  • No personal information is collected or tracked.
  • No cookies or trackers are used.
  • Your saved timestamps and notes are stored locally in your browser.

Permissions

We only request the permissions required for core functionality:

  • Access to YouTube pages — to allow bookmarking.
  • Storage — to save your timestamps and settings locally.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be published here. Your continued use of the extension signifies your acceptance of the policy.
